Healthcare clinical applications

Lattice Health’s clinical applications support the clinician and the patient throughout their healthcare journey. Our clinical applications help practitioners deliver knowledge-driven care—informed by data from electronic health records (EHRs) and across the continuum—to improve the quality of care, help drive revenue, and boost clinical and operational efficiency.

Extend coordinated care across the continuum with clinical applications

Lattice Health aims to improve the clinical and patient experience with knowledge-driven care and specialty-based workflows using an open and interoperable system that enables the exchange of information between homes, health systems, and communities.
